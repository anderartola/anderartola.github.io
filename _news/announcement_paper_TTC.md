We have a new preprint! In this [paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.21839) we introduce *test-time compute games*, a game-theoretic framework for analyzing how LLM providers choose the amount of test-time compute deployed by their models. Building on this framework, we show that current LLM-as-a-service markets are socially inefficient, and we propose a forward-looking auction mechanism that is provably socially optimal.
