Hi! I'm Ander, a PhD. student at the [Max Planck Institute for Software Systems](https://www.mpi-sws.org), under the supervision of [Dr. Manuel Gomez Rodriguez](https://people.mpi-sws.org/~manuelgr/) and as part of the IMPRS-TRUST program. Previously, I obtained my masters in Physics at Heidelberg University under the supervision of [Dr. Sarah Bosman](https://sites.google.com/view/sarahbosman). I graduated in 2023 from the Complutense University of Madrid in Mathematics and Theoretical Physics.
